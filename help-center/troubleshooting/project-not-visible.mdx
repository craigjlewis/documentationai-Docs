---
title: Project Not Visible After Creation
description: Steps if your new CJL_Org project isn't showing up.
---

## What’s Happening?

- New project doesn’t appear on the dashboard.
- Project list doesn’t update after creation.

## Quick Fixes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the Page">
    Sometimes, new projects appear after reloading your browser window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-login">
    Log out, then log back in to refresh your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    Make sure you have access to the project or were added as a member under Project Settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">
If the issue continues, contact support at support@cjlorg.com.
</Callout>
