## Steps to Begin

1. Sign up at `https://cjlorg.com`.
2. Verify your email address.
3. Log in with your credentials.
4. Click “Create Project” to start organizing documentation.
5. Invite team members under Project Settings if needed.

<Callout kind="tip">
Use the left sidebar to navigate to your projects at any time.
</Callout>
